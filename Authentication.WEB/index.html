InsuredTraveling
Android application for buying insurance policies.

Our solution

TravellingInsured is project which is a standalone platform for online sales of insurance policies which includes both mobile apps and web administration panel. The mobile app is friendly and allows users to buy insurance on the go. The administrative panel allows administration of the sales, but also drilling down reports and administration of the sales.

To make it easier for our clients to buy and manage insurance policies.

The application is offering the insurance companies the opportunity to get to know their clients more deeply and to build better marketing campaigns.
